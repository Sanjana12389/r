Camel

Chameleons

  • The name chameleon comes from ancient Greek and Latin and means "ground lion" or "lion on the ground.
  • Chameleons are known to be a very intelligent species of lizard.
  • They can change the color of their skin using skin crystals.

Bears

  • There are a total of 8 bear species.
  • Bears have the largest and most complex brains in comparison to other land mammals their size and they rely on this brain for a number of behaviors.
  • Polar bears have the speed of 25 mph.



Cicadas

  • Cicadas do not have jaws so they cant eat or bite.
  • They prune mature trees, aerate the soil, and once they die, their bodies serve as an important source of nitrogen for growing trees.
  • Rain makes it difficult for cicadas to fly.

Pandas

  • A recent calculation has proven that the Giant Panda species are not cosidered endagered anymore!
  • Pandas can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo everyday.
  • They have different eyes as compared to a regular bear.

Pigeons

  • Pigeons can move their wings ten times per second and maintain heartbeats at the rate of 600 times per minute.
  • A pigeon will likely remember you if you ever come close to one.
  • Pigeons actually carry the highest number of diseases in comparison to any other bird species.
